HOW TO PREPARE EGUSI SOUP

Egusi soup is one of my favourite soup. Egusi seeds are rich in fat and protein, it add more nutrients into whatever meal you choose to make with it.
There are different methods of making egusi soup but, this is how I make mine. Checkout my recipe.



INGREDIENTS

  • Egusi seeds (melon)
  • Onion
  • Fresh pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Meat (or any of choice)
  • Cow skin (Ponmo)
  • Stockfish
  • Dried Fish
  • Locust seed (Optional)
  • Pumpkin leaves (or any leave of your choice)
  • Seosoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Palm oil

BEFORE YOU START COOKING

  1. Cook meat and ponmo using seasoning cubes, salt, water, and onions (Stock).
  2. Cook stockfish together with meat or Soak your stockfish.
  3. Blend egusi with onions and add little water to form a thick paste.
  4. Debone dried fish after soaking.

COOKING PROCEDURES - (FRYING METHOD)

  1. Place pot on medium heat, add palm oil and heat for about 3 minutes (Do not bleach oil) add chopped onions.
    Then add pepper mix, locust beans and stir to combine and fry for 3mins.
  2. At this point add blended crayfish, dried fish, stir and allow to fry for 1min.
  3. At this point, bring in the Egusi paste, stir and allow to fry for 10mins. Make sure you stir gently each minute so it doesn’t burn.
  4. At this point add in your cooked meat, stockfish and pomo (Stock), stir and cover to simmer for 2mins.
  5. At this point, bring in the pumpkin leaves or any vegetable of ur choice, stir and simmer for about 5 minutes.
    Adjust the seasoning if necessary and the meat stock water if it’s not enough to cook the soup. Then turn of heat immediately.
  6. Egusi soup is ready to be served with any swallow of your chioce
Preparation Time Cooking Time Total Time
15min 35mins 50mins
